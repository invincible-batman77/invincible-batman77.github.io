Vision

Salenderbucchi Aerospace operates at the intersection of engineering, long-term strategy, and human expansion. Our systems are designed not just to reach destinations, but to sustain life beyond Earth.

From autonomous navigation to orbital infrastructure, every layer of our work is focused on reliability, scalability, and permanence.

The objective is clear: transform humanity into a multi-planetary civilization.

Mars Program

The Mars Program is structured as a multi-decade initiative focused on gradual expansion and long-term sustainability.

Phase I — Autonomous cargo missions delivering essential infrastructure including energy systems, communication relays, and robotic construction units.

Phase II — Deployment of self-sustaining habitats designed for environmental resilience, energy independence, and resource utilization.

Phase III — Human arrival, expansion of settlements, and development of a permanent, evolving planetary ecosystem.
